Problem Statement

Fleet and finance teams lack a fast, conversational way to answer: "Did we have fuel theft last week?", "Which vehicles had drops with no matching card transaction?", and "Where did this card get used?" without digging through dashboards or writing reports.

Who is this for:

  • Product / fleet leads using Claude Desktop (or another MCP host) who want to query theft and fuel-card behaviour via chat.
  • Ops / fleet managers who need quick theft summaries and optional Slack alerts.
  • Finance interested in reconciling tank drops with card transactions and hold requests.

Solution

Expose Geotab (and ACE, fuel card and Slack integrations) as tools to an AI. The assistant can run theft detection, compare tank drops to card data, geocode locations, and optionally post alerts—so product and ops leads can ask in plain language and get summarized, actionable answers.
