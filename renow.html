Renow – AI Renovation Copilot

Product Manager & Founder | 2025

Overview

Renow is an AI-powered copilot that helps homeowners, renters, and DIYers plan and manage renovations confidently.

Problem Statement

New homeowners, renters and DIYers struggle to complete renovation projects because the current market makes designing, sourcing products, and finding reliable installers confusing, expensive and fragmented, leading to abandoned projects and a lack of trust in managing renovation by themselves.

User Research

  • 12 customer interviews across 3 homeowner segments
  • Key insight: “I didn’t even know where to start — the moment I Googled tiles, I felt overwhelmed.”

Market Analysis

  • DIY renovation market projected to hit $XXB by 2026
  • Competitive tools (Houzz, Thumbtack) lack end-to-end simplicity

Value Proposition

Renow helps you design, budget, source, and plan home upgrades — all in one assistant-powered experience.

MVP Build

  • Used Lovable to build MVP prototype
  • Key features: AI project planner, local sourcing, timeline tracker

Initial MVP built using Lovable showcasing renovation planner UI.

OKRs & Go-To-Market

  • OKR: Launch waitlist + acquire 100 beta users in 3 months
  • GTM: TikTok/IG short-form content + newsletter partnerships

Results & Learnings

Early feedback from 25 users showed high interest. Learned that transparency in budgeting is a critical user trust factor. Planning to integrate cost estimator in v2.
